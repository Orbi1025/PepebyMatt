MATT FURIE - PEPE

Artist: Matt Furie

Exhibition: Tuff Crowd

Image Size: 6 in. x 4 in. Frame Size: 7 in. x 5 in. Medium: Ink and colored pencil on bristol

On May 6, 2017, on Free Comic Book Day, it was announced that Furie had killed Pepe off in response to the character's continued use as a hate symbol. Pepe by Matt Furie is a piece featured in the 2019 art expo "Tuff Crowd" that was created to replace the original, green Pepe.
